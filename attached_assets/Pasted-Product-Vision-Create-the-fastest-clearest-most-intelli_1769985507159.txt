Product Vision

Create the fastest, clearest, most intelligent NFL companion experience for live games, teams, players, and league narratives — optimized for real-time consumption, personal relevance, and broadcast-grade polish.

This product does not replace broadcast — it augments it.

⸻

2. Goals & Success Criteria

Business Goals
	•	Increase daily active usage during live games
	•	Become the default second-screen experience
	•	Enable sponsorship-safe, non-intrusive monetization
	•	Support future betting, fantasy, and media integrations

User Goals
	•	Instantly understand what’s happening now
	•	Never miss important moments for favorite teams/players
	•	Consume highlights, stats, and insights with zero friction

Success Metrics (KPIs)
	•	DAU/MAU ≥ 45% during season
	•	Median time-to-live-score < 1s
	•	Avg taps to core info ≤ 2
	•	Notification opt-in rate ≥ 65%
	•	Crash-free sessions ≥ 99.8%

⸻

3. Target Users

Primary
	•	NFL fans (casual → hardcore)
	•	Live game viewers (TV, stadium, mobile)

Secondary
	•	Fantasy players
	•	Sports bettors (future-ready)
	•	Media professionals & analysts

⸻

4. Core Product Principles (2026 Standard)
	1.	Real-time first
	2.	Personalized by default
	3.	Readable at a glance
	4.	Predictive, not reactive
	5.	Broadcast-level polish
	6.	Zero dead ends

⸻

5. Core Features & Requirements

⸻

5.1 Home Feed (AI-Driven)

Purpose: Immediate relevance on launch

Requirements
	•	Personalized feed based on:
	•	Favorite teams
	•	Favorite players
	•	Current live games
	•	Time of day / game phase
	•	Auto-reorders during:
	•	Kickoff windows
	•	Final quarters
	•	Overtime
	•	“What you missed” recap post-game

2026 UX Expectations
	•	Predictive cards (e.g. “Your team starts in 12 mins”)
	•	Dynamic layouts (no static homepage)

⸻

5.2 Live Scoreboard

Purpose: Fastest league-wide awareness

Requirements
	•	All games view:
	•	Score
	•	Quarter
	•	Clock
	•	Possession indicator
	•	Game state (live / final / upcoming)
	•	Filters:
	•	Favorites
	•	Division
	•	Prime-time
	•	Offline fallback with timestamp

UX Requirements
	•	Readable in <2 seconds
	•	No horizontal scrolling
	•	One-tap entry to Game Center

⸻

5.3 Game Center (Critical Feature)

Purpose: Second-screen command center

Requirements
	•	Live score & clock
	•	Down & distance visualization
	•	Drive charts
	•	Scoring summary
	•	Key player stats (contextual)
	•	Live win probability (future-safe)

2026 Enhancements
	•	Micro-animations on scoring plays
	•	Context-aware stat surfacing
	•	Pinch to expand stats / plays

⸻

5.4 Teams Hub

Purpose: Persistent team identity

Requirements
	•	Team overview
	•	Schedule
	•	Roster
	•	Injury report
	•	Standings snapshot
	•	Recent form (last 5 games)

UX Requirements
	•	Team color system (accessibility-safe)
	•	Sticky headers
	•	Clear season context (Week / Record)

⸻

5.5 Player Profiles

Purpose: Humanize stats

Requirements
	•	Player bio & photo
	•	Season + recent stats
	•	Game-by-game log
	•	Injury status
	•	Team & position context

2026 UX
	•	Stat trend visuals
	•	Highlight linking
	•	Tap-from-anywhere access

⸻

5.6 Standings & Playoff Picture

Requirements
	•	Conference & division views
	•	Tiebreaker indicators
	•	Clinched / eliminated states
	•	Playoff projection (future-ready)

UX
	•	Zero confusion
	•	Always labeled clearly
	•	No scrolling traps

⸻

5.7 Highlights & Media

Requirements
	•	Short-form highlights
	•	Game recap clips
	•	Condensed game (rights-dependent)
	•	Vertical & horizontal formats

UX
	•	Inline playback
	•	No blocking modals
	•	Progressive loading

⸻

5.8 Notifications System

Purpose: Retention without annoyance

Requirements
	•	Opt-in by:
	•	Team
	•	Player
	•	Event type
	•	Smart throttling
	•	Lock-screen live score updates

2026 Enhancements
	•	Context-aware notification timing
	•	Haptic differentiation (iOS / Android)

⸻

5.9 Search & Discovery

Requirements
	•	Global search:
	•	Teams
	•	Players
	•	Games
	•	Voice search (OS-level)
	•	Recent searches

⸻

6. Onboarding & Identity

Requirements
	•	Passkey / biometric login
	•	Progressive onboarding:
	1.	Pick teams
	2.	Pick players
	3.	Notification preferences
	•	Skip-friendly, fast

⸻

7. Accessibility (Non-Negotiable)
	•	Dynamic type scaling
	•	Screen reader support
	•	High-contrast mode
	•	Large tap targets
	•	Color-blind-safe palettes

⸻

8. Performance & Reliability

Targets
	•	App launch < 2s
	•	Live update latency < 1s
	•	Graceful degradation on poor networks
	•	Cached fallback states

⸻

9. Monetization & Sponsorship (UX-Safe)

Allowed
	•	Inline sponsor cards
	•	Branded stats modules
	•	Premium insights tier (future)

Disallowed
	•	Full-screen interstitials during live play
	•	Blocking ads on score views

⸻

10. Analytics & Telemetry

Tracked
	•	Screen flows
	•	Live game engagement
	•	Notification interactions
	•	Drop-off points
	•	Performance metrics

⸻

11. Non-Goals (Explicit)
	•	No fantasy league management (v1)
	•	No betting execution (future-ready only)
	•	No social chat rooms (moderation risk)
